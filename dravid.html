Interesting Statstics

Rahul Dravid retired as the third highest capped player in test cricket.

Dravid played 94 consecutive Tests since his debut in 1996, without missing a single match.

Dravid retired as the third highest run getter of all time in Test cricket.

Dravid has scored 7,690 runs at an average of 53.03 away from home.

Dravid is the first international cricketer to have scored more than 10,000 runs batting at no. 3 position.

Dravid holds the world record for taking most number of catches as a non-wicketkeeper fielder in Test Cricket.

Dravid has been involved in 2nd most number of partnerships (738).

Dravid has more hundred run partnerships and more fifty run partnerships than any other batsman.

The only batsman to have been involved in two ODI partnerships exceeding 300 runs.

Has the record of not being dismissed on duck for the most number of consecutive ODI matches.
